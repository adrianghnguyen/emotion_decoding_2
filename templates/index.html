A feelings wheel is a visual tool designed to help individuals identify and articulate their emotions with greater precision. It typically consists of a central emotion category, such as "anger" or "joy," which then branches out into more specific feelings within that category. The wheel provides a hierarchical structure, allowing users to navigate through layers of emotions to pinpoint their current emotional state accurately.

  • Enhances emotional intelligence by helping us recognize and understand our emotions
  • Makes it easier for us to precisely communicate the state of our feelings, contributing to healthier interpersonal relationships.
  • Supports the development of healthier coping mechanisms through a nuanced understanding of emotions.
